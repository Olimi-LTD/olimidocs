## How It Works
Olimi seamlessly integrates its advanced AI call center technology with your existing communication infrastructure, revolutionizing how you manage and optimize your telecommunications.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    id1(Olimi AI) <--> |SIP Trunk|id3(Your PBX)
    id3 <--> id4(Your Extensions)
    id4 <--> id5(SIP Trunk)
    id4 <--> id6(PSTN)
    id4 <--> id7(GSM)
    id4 <--> id8(WebRTC)
    id4 <--> id9(VoIP)

```

Our innovative system connects Olimi AI to your SIP Trunks. This setup allows for seamless integration with various communication channels, including SIP Trunks, PSTN, GSM, WebRTC, and VoIP, providing you with a versatile and powerful communication solution.
